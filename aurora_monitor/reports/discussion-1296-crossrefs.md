## Overview

and r/aurora. Within those Reddit posts, community members linked to Aurora Forums
topics and YouTube videos. The discussion body reported 482 cross-references;
this extraction found **59 unique Aurora Forums URLs** and **21 unique YouTube URLs**
(80 total unique URLs) from two sources:

1. **Discussion comments** (20 triage pages) -- links embedded in Reddit post/comment excerpts
2. **Issue comments** (44 sibling verification issues) -- links in Reddit community reference sections

The gap between 80 unique URLs and 482 reported cross-references is explained by:
- Many Reddit posts reference the same forum topic (deduplication)
- Some URLs were truncated in the triage excerpts (comment text was cut at ~80 chars)
- The 482 count may include Reddit-to-Reddit cross-references not extracted here
